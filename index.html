FAQs

Can I customise my order?

Yes, absolutely! You can customise your order by choosing your preferred colour scheme and adding an edible image if you wish. We specialise in vintage-style cakes, and while custom details are always welcome, all designs are created within our signature style. Feel free to enquire about any special requests and we’ll be happy to let you know what’s possible.
